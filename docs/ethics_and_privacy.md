# Ethics and privacy

- Public visibility of posts on Weibo does not imply unrestricted redistribution rights.
- This repository does not publish raw post text, usernames, or account identifiers.
- Deleted and inaccessible posts are outside the survivor corpus and are not redistributed.
- Engagement outcomes describe interaction within accessible posts only; they are not measures of algorithmic exposure.
- LLM labels are fallible structured measurements; validation metrics (including Indirect-Mixed recall = 0.500) are reported in Appendix B.
- Test fixtures use synthetic placeholders only.
